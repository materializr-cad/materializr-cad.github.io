MATERIALIZR Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective 18 June 2026

Materializr does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The application runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Materializr has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or telemetry. It does not track your activity and does not contain advertising or third-party SDKs that collect data.

Network access

The mobile builds of Materializr — both iOS and Android — make no network connections. The app works fully offline on every platform.

Your files

The designs and files you create stay on your device. Opening and saving files uses your device's system file picker (the Storage Access Framework on Android, the document picker on iOS), so the app only ever touches the specific files you choose — it requests no broad storage permission. Files leave your device only if you yourself export or share them.

Children

Materializr is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised at github.com/materializr-cad/materializr/issues.
